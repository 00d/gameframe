ICEWYRM
Resembling wingless, serpentine dragons formed of jagged
ice and shot through with veins of nearly frozen water, these
elementals dwell within icebergs and enjoy striking out at
passing ships or creatures. They’re especially common in frigid
stretches of ocean in the Plane of Water, where icebergs cluster
together into enormous islands of ice.
