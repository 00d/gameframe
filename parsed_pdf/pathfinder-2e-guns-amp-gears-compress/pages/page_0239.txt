Gear Up and Throw Down!
When sword and spell aren’t enough to win the day, it’s time to power up your
game with clockwork gears, lightning coils, and black powder! Unravel the
secrets of clockworks with the new inventor class or blow away your opposition
as a firearm-wielding gunslinger. In addition to new classes, this book holds a
plethora of archetypes, backgrounds, vehicles, siege weapons, gadgets, and the new
automaton ancestry, all ready to expand your game with options for battlefields
large and small. Revolutionize your game with Pathfinder Guns & Gears!
