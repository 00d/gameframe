Sea devils are prone to mutations,
the most common of which is the
manifestation of two additional
arms. These mutated sea devils
almost always rise to power as
barons. Other mutations occur as
well, but few are as strange as
those that result in sea devils who
look almost like aquatic elves—
save for a row of sharp teeth in
their mouths.
