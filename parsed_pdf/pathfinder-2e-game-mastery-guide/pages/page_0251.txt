GLOSSARY AND INDEX
This appendix contains page references for key terms
appearing in this book, partial definitions for many rules
and concepts, and full definitions for the traits introduced
in this book. Definitions of other traits used in this book
can be found in the Glossary and Index beginning on
page 628 of the Core Rulebook.
