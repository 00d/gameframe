Class Kits
If you want to quickly decide how to spend your starting
money on what your class needs, start with one of these kits.
Note than an adventurer’s pack, which is included in each kit,
contains a backpack, a bedroll, two belt pouches, 10 pieces of
chalk, flint and steel, 50 feet of rope, 2 weeks’ rations, soap,
5 torches, and a waterskin.
