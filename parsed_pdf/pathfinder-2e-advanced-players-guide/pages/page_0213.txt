Spell Lists
These lists contain the spells for each tradition, including
cantrips. (Focus spells appear on pages 228–239.) A
superscript “H” indicates a spell has extra effects when
heightened, and a spell whose rarity is greater than
common has a superscript with the first letter of that rarity.
The abbreviation in parentheses indicates a spell’s school.
