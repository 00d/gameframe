Campaign Overview
4
by Ron Lundeen
Learn about the campaign’s main villain, history, and themes.
Otari
8
by James Jacobs
The sleepy seaside town of Otari is the heroes’ main base of operations
throughout the campaign. This article describes its residents, adventure
hooks, backgrounds, and opportunities for downtime.
Chapter 1: A Light in the Fog
20
by James Jacobs
Wrin Sivinxi suspects something dangerous in the ruins of Gauntlight Keep
and asks the heroes to investigate the ruins and its strange lighthouse.
Chapter 2: The Forgotten Dungeon
36
by James Jacobs
Gauntlight’s dread beam animates the dead in Otari’s graveyard, and the
heroes must help quell the undead uprising and return to the dungeon.
Chapter 3: Cult of the Canker
52
by James Jacobs
The heroes explore a sprawling library now inhabited by a group of scholarly
and sinister ghouls who call themselves the Cult of the Canker.
Chapter 4: Long Dream the Dead
70
by James Jacobs
After the heroes encounter the ghost of Otari Ilvashti, they receive a dire
warning about what lies even deeper in the Abomination Vaults.
Chapter 5: Into the Training Grounds
84
by Vanessa Hoskins
The heroes recover four icons from Otari’s founders to access deeper dungeon
levels and meet townspeople with surprising connections to the dungeon.
Chapter 6: Experiments in Flesh
106
by Vanessa Hoskins
In old alchemical laboratories, the heroes can foil a seugathi’s schemes.
Chapter 7: Soul Keepers
126
by Vanessa Hoskins
A contract devil named Urevian must fulﬁll a centuries-old agreement, but
the heroes might strike a deal with the devil to save the town.
Chapter 8: Decaying Gardens
144
by Stephen Radney-MacFarland
The heroes encounter a strange caligni cult and a cabal of undead gnomes.
Chapter 9: On the Hunt
166
by Stephen Radney-MacFarland
In the enormous Hunting Grounds level, the heroes can learn the secret to
defeating Belcorra and ﬁnd the required fulcrum lenses.
