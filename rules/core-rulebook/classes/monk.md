# Monk

The monk uses ki to enhance their body and mind, mastering unarmed combat.

## Key Ability
Dexterity or Strength

## Hit Points
8

## Initial Proficiencies
- Perception: Expert
- Saving Throws: Fortitude Expert, Reflex Expert, Will Expert
- Skills: Trained in Acrobatics, Stealth, plus additional skills equal to 4 + Int modifier
- Attacks: Trained in simple weapons, unarmed attacks
- Defenses: Trained in unarmored defense
- Class DC: Trained

## Monk Feats
- Flurry of Blows (1st): Two Strikes as one action.
- Powerful Fist (1st).

## Class Features
- **Flurry of Blows** (1st): Extra attacks.
- **Ki Spells** (1st): Monk ki spells.
