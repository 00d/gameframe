# Fighter

The fighter is a master of martial combat, using weapons and armor to protect allies and vanquish foes.

## Key Ability
Strength or Dexterity

## Hit Points
10

## Initial Proficiencies
- Perception: Expert
- Saving Throws: Fortitude Expert, Reflex Expert
- Skills: Trained in Athletics, plus additional skills equal to 3 + Int modifier
- Attacks: Trained in simple and martial weapons, unarmed attacks
- Defenses: Trained in light, medium, and heavy armor, unarmored defense
- Class DC: Trained

## Fighter Feats
- Attack of Opportunity (1st)
- Shield Block (1st)
- And more at higher levels.

## Class Features
- **Weapon Expertise** (5th): Simple and martial weapons to expert.
- **Armor Expertise** (11th): Light, medium, heavy armor to expert.
- **Weapon Mastery** (13th): Simple and martial to master.
- **Armor Mastery** (19th): All armor to master.

## Fighter Archetypes
- Archer, etc.
