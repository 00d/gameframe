# Rogue

The rogue excels at stealth, traps, and opportunistic strikes.

## Key Ability
Dexterity

## Hit Points
8

## Initial Proficiencies
- Perception: Expert
- Saving Throws: Fortitude Trained, Reflex Expert, Will Trained
- Skills: Trained in Stealth, Thievery, plus additional skills equal to 4 + Int modifier
- Attacks: Trained in simple weapons, rapier, sap, shortbow, unarmed attacks
- Defenses: Trained in light armor, unarmored defense
- Class DC: Trained

## Racket
Choose a racket (e.g., Thief, Swashbuckler).

## Rogue Feats
- Sneak Attack (1st): Extra damage on flat-footed foes.
- Trapfinding (1st).

## Class Features
- **Sneak Attack** (1st): Precision damage.
- **Rogue's Racket** (1st): Special abilities.
