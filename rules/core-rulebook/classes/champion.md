# Champion

The champion is a divine warrior bound to a deity, wielding faith and arms to protect allies and smite foes.

## Key Ability
Strength or Dexterity

## Hit Points
10

## Initial Proficiencies
- Perception: Trained
- Saving Throws: Fortitude Expert, Reflex Trained, Will Expert
- Skills: Trained in Religion, plus additional skills equal to 3 + Int modifier
- Attacks: Trained in simple and martial weapons, unarmed attacks
- Defenses: Trained in light, medium, and heavy armor, unarmored defense
- Class DC: Trained

## Deity and Cause
Choose a deity and cause (e.g., Paladin for Lawful Good).

## Champion Feats
- Divine Ally (1st): Weapon, shield, or steed.
- And more.

## Class Features
- **Champion's Reaction** (1st): Retributive Strike.
- **Devotion Spells** (1st): Divine spells.
- **Exalt** (6th): Empower attacks.
