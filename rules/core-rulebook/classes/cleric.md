# Cleric

The cleric draws power from faith, casting divine spells and supporting allies.

## Key Ability
Wisdom

## Hit Points
8

## Initial Proficiencies
- Perception: Trained
- Saving Throws: Fortitude Trained, Reflex Trained, Will Expert
- Skills: Trained in Religion, plus additional skills equal to 2 + Int modifier
- Attacks: Trained in simple weapons
- Defenses: Trained in light and medium armor, unarmored defense
- Class DC: Trained

## Deity and Doctrine
Choose a deity and doctrine (e.g., Warpriest).

## Cleric Feats
- Deadly Simplicity (1st): Deadly trait on simple weapons.
- Divine Spellcasting (1st).

## Class Features
- **Divine Font** (1st): Heal or Harm.
- **Divine Spellcasting** (1st): Prepare divine spells.
