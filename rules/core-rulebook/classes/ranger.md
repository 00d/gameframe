# Ranger

The ranger is a skilled hunter and explorer, adept at tracking and ranged combat.

## Key Ability
Dexterity

## Hit Points
10

## Initial Proficiencies
- Perception: Expert
- Saving Throws: Fortitude Expert, Reflex Expert, Will Trained
- Skills: Trained in Survival, Stealth, plus additional skills equal to 4 + Int modifier
- Attacks: Trained in simple and martial weapons, unarmed attacks
- Defenses: Trained in light and medium armor, unarmored defense
- Class DC: Trained

## Hunter's Edge
Choose an edge (e.g., Precision, Outwit).

## Ranger Feats
- Hunter's Aim (1st): Ignore cover.
- Crossbow Ace (1st).

## Class Features
- **Hunt Prey** (1st): Mark a foe for bonuses.
- **Hunter's Edge** (1st): Special abilities.
