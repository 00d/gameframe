# Wizard

The wizard masters arcane magic through study and spellbooks.

## Key Ability
Intelligence

## Hit Points
6

## Initial Proficiencies
- Perception: Trained
- Saving Throws: Fortitude Trained, Reflex Trained, Will Expert
- Skills: Trained in Arcana, plus additional skills equal to 2 + Int modifier
- Attacks: Trained in simple weapons
- Defenses: Trained in unarmored defense
- Class DC: Trained

## Wizard School
Choose a school (e.g., Abjuration, Conjuration).

## Wizard Feats
- Arcane Spellcasting (1st): Prepare arcane spells.
- Spellbook (1st).

## Class Features
- **Arcane Spellcasting** (1st): Prepare spells from spellbook.
- **Wizard School** (1st): School abilities.
