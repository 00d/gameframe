# Sorcerer

The sorcerer channels innate magical bloodline power.

## Key Ability
Charisma

## Hit Points
6

## Initial Proficiencies
- Perception: Trained
- Saving Throws: Fortitude Trained, Reflex Trained, Will Expert
- Skills: Trained in Arcana, Occultism, plus additional skills equal to 2 + Int modifier
- Attacks: Trained in simple weapons
- Defenses: Trained in unarmored defense
- Class DC: Trained

## Bloodline
Choose a bloodline (e.g., Aberrant, Angelic).

## Sorcerer Feats
- Bloodline Spell (1st).
- Spellcasting (1st).

## Class Features
- **Bloodline** (1st): Innate abilities.
- **Spellcasting** (1st): Spontaneous arcane or divine spells.
