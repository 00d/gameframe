# Barbarian

The barbarian channels primal rage to unleash devastating fury in battle.

## Key Ability
Strength

## Hit Points
12

## Initial Proficiencies
- Perception: Expert
- Saving Throws: Fortitude Expert, Will Expert
- Skills: Trained in Athletics, plus additional skills equal to 3 + Int modifier
- Attacks: Trained in simple and martial weapons, unarmed attacks
- Defenses: Trained in light and medium armor, unarmored defense
- Class DC: Trained

## Rage
Enter a rage as a free action. While raging: +2 damage, resistance to physical damage equal to half level (min 1), AC penalty -1 (unless Animal Skin).

## Instincts
Choose an instinct: Animal, Dragon, Fury, Giant, Spirit, Superstition, etc. Provides special abilities.
