# Classes

Classes define your character's profession and abilities. Choose from:

- Alchemist: Brews potions and bombs.
- Barbarian: Channels rage for fury.
- Champion: Divine warrior.
- Cleric: Faith-based spellcaster.
- Druid: Nature magic user.
- Fighter: Martial combatant.
- Monk: Unarmed ki master.
- Ranger: Wilderness hunter.
- Rogue: Stealthy trickster.
- Sorcerer: Innate magic wielder.
- Wizard: Studied arcane mage.

See individual class files for details.
