# Chapter 2: Ancestries

Ancestry represents your character's heritage and physical traits. Choose an ancestry that fits your character's backstory and mechanics.

## Common Ancestries

### Dwarf

Dwarves are stout, sturdy folk who live in mountains and underground. They are known for their craftsmanship, resilience, and grudges.

- **Hit Points**: 10
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 20 feet
- **Ability Boosts**: Constitution, Wisdom; Free: Constitution
- **Ability Flaw**: Charisma
- **Languages**: Common, Dwarven (additional equal to Intelligence modifier if positive: Gnomish, Goblin, Jotun, Orcish, Terran, Undercommon, etc.)
- **Traits**: Dwarf, Humanoid
- **Darkvision**: You can see in darkness and dim light just as well as you can see in bright light, though your vision in darkness is in black and white.
- **Ancestry Feats**: Stonecunning, Dwarven Weapon Familiarity, Boulder Roll, etc.

#### Dwarf Heritages

- **Ancient-Blooded Dwarf**: Gain poison resistance equal to half your level (min 1), and +1 vs. poison saves.
- **Death Warden Dwarf**: If you succeed on a save vs. necromancy, get a critical success instead.
- **Forge Dwarf**: Fire resistance equal to half your level (min 1), treat environmental heat as one step less extreme.
- **Rock Dwarf**: +2 circumstance bonus to Fortitude or Reflex DC vs. Shove or Trip. Reduce forced movement by half (min 10 ft).
- **Strong-Blooded Dwarf**: Poison resistance equal to half your level (min 1), successful saves vs. poison reduce stage by 2 (1 for virulent).

### Elf

Elves are graceful, long-lived beings attuned to nature and magic. They live in forests and have keen senses.

- **Hit Points**: 6
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 feet
- **Ability Boosts**: Dexterity, Intelligence; Free: Dexterity
- **Ability Flaw**: Constitution
- **Languages**: Common, Elven (additional equal to Intelligence modifier if positive: Celestial, Draconic, Gnoll, Gnomish, Goblin, Orcish, Sylvan, etc.)
- **Traits**: Elf, Humanoid
- **Low-Light Vision**: You can see in dim light as though it were bright light, so you ignore the concealed condition due to dim light.
- **Ancestry Feats**: Elven Weapon Familiarity, Ancestral Longevity, etc.

#### Elf Heritages

- **Arctic Elf**: Cold resistance equal to half your level (min 1), treat environmental cold as one step less extreme.
- **Cavern Elf**: Gain darkvision.
- **Seer Elf**: +1 circumstance bonus to Recall Knowledge.
- **Whisper Elf**: +1 circumstance bonus to Stealth checks vs. hearing, and Perception checks using hearing.
- **Woodland Elf**: Ignore difficult terrain from underbrush, trees, etc.

### Gnome

Gnomes are curious, inventive folk who love tinkering and magic. They are small but full of energy.

- **Hit Points**: 8
- **Size**: Small
- **Speed**: 25 feet
- **Ability Boosts**: Constitution, Charisma; Free: Constitution
- **Ability Flaw**: Strength
- **Languages**: Common, Gnomish
- **Traits**: Gnome, Humanoid
- **Low-Light Vision**: See in dim light as bright light.
- **Ancestry Feats**: Animal Accomplice, Gnome Weapon Familiarity, etc.

### Goblin

Goblins are small, energetic creatures known for their love of fire, explosions, and mischief.

- **Hit Points**: 6
- **Size**: Small
- **Speed**: 25 feet
- **Ability Boosts**: Dexterity, Charisma; Free: Dexterity
- **Ability Flaw**: Wisdom
- **Languages**: Common, Goblin
- **Traits**: Goblin, Humanoid
- **Darkvision**: See in darkness up to 60 feet as dim light.
- **Ancestry Feats**: Goblin Weapon Familiarity, Junk Tinker, etc.

### Halfling

Halflings are cheerful, lucky folk who live in communities and value friendship.

- **Hit Points**: 6
- **Size**: Small
- **Speed**: 25 feet
- **Ability Boosts**: Dexterity, Wisdom; Free: Dexterity
- **Ability Flaw**: Strength
- **Languages**: Common, Halfling
- **Traits**: Halfling, Humanoid
- **Keen Eyes**: +2 Perception for seeing things at a distance.
- **Ancestry Feats**: Halfling Weapon Familiarity, Sure Feet, etc.

### Human

Humans are versatile and adaptable, capable of great deeds or villainy.

- **Hit Points**: 8
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 25 feet
- **Ability Boosts**: Two free boosts
- **Languages**: Common, one extra
- **Traits**: Human, Humanoid
- **Ancestry Feats**: General Training, Natural Ambition, etc.

## Uncommon Ancestries

- **Half-Elf**: Blend of human and elf traits.
- **Half-Orc**: Strong and resilient.
- And others.

Each ancestry provides mechanical benefits and roleplaying opportunities.
