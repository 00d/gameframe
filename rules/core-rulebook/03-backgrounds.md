# Chapter 3: Backgrounds

Backgrounds describe your character's upbringing, training, and experiences before becoming an adventurer. Each background provides two ability boosts, trained proficiency in two skills, and a background feat.

## Choosing a Background

Select a background that fits your character's story. You can choose from the list below or create a custom one with GM approval.

## Common Backgrounds

### Acrobat

You performed in circuses, on streets, or in theaters, showcasing feats of agility and balance.

- **Ability Boosts**: Dexterity, Charisma
- **Skill Training**: Acrobatics, Performance
- **Background Feat**: Steady Balance

### Animal Whisperer

You have a way with animals, perhaps as a trainer, breeder, or naturalist.

- **Ability Boosts**: Wisdom, Charisma
- **Skill Training**: Nature, Survival
- **Background Feat**: Train Animal

### Artisan

You are skilled in crafting, perhaps as a blacksmith, carpenter, or jeweler.

- **Ability Boosts**: Strength, Intelligence
- **Skill Training**: Crafting, Lore (relevant)
- **Background Feat**: Specialty Crafting

### Artist

You create art, such as paintings, sculptures, or performances.

- **Ability Boosts**: Dexterity, Charisma

### Barkeep

### Bounty Hunter
