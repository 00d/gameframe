# Playing the Game

## Encounter Modes
- **Exploration**: Travel, search, interact with environment.
- **Downtime**: Days or weeks for crafting, training, etc.
- **Combat**: Rounds of actions.

## Actions
- Single Action [1]
- Two Actions [2]
- Three Actions [3]
- Reaction [R]
- Free Action [F]

## Basic Actions
- Strike: Attack with weapon.
- Stride: Move.
- Interact: Manipulate object.

## Checks and DCs
Roll d20 + modifiers vs. DC.

## Initiative
Dexterity check to determine turn order.
