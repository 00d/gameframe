# Skills

Skills represent your character's training and natural aptitudes. Each skill has a proficiency rank: Untrained, Trained, Expert, Master, Legendary.

## Acrobatics (Dex)
Balance, tumble, squeeze through tight spaces.

## Arcana (Int)
Recall knowledge about magic, spells, magical traditions.

## Athletics (Str)
Climb, swim, jump, grapple.

## Crafting (Int)
Create items, repair, identify.

## Deception (Cha)
Lie, disguise, impersonate.

## Diplomacy (Cha)
Gather info, make impressions, request favors.

## Intimidation (Cha)
Coerce, demoralize.

## Lore (Int)
Specific knowledge, e.g., Warfare Lore.

## Medicine (Wis)
Treat wounds, recall diseases.

## Nature (Wis)
Recall plants, animals, weather.

## Occultism (Int)
Recall occult lore, identify curses.

## Performance (Cha)
Perform arts, earn income.

## Religion (Wis)
Recall religious knowledge.

## Society (Int)
Recall history, culture, nobility.

## Stealth (Dex)
Hide, sneak, avoid notice.

## Survival (Wis)
Track, forage, navigate wilderness.

## Thievery (Dex)
Pick locks, disable traps, steal.
