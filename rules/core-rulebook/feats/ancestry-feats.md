# Ancestry Feats

Ancestry feats are tied to your ancestry.

## Dwarf
- Stonecunning: Bonus to notice stonework.
- Dwarven Weapon Familiarity: Trained in dwarven weapons.

## Elf
- Elven Weapon Familiarity: Trained in elven weapons.
- Ancestral Longevity: +2 to saves vs. diseases and poisons.
